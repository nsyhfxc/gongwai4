\section{Translation}

\subsection{Unit 1}

\begin{question}{课后翻译}

城乡一体化（urban-rural integration）是我国现代化和城市化发展的一个新阶段。其目的是通过对城乡发展进行统筹规划，并通过体制改革和政策调整，破除城乡二元经济结构（the urban-rural dual economic structure），从而实现城乡在政策上平等、在产业发展模式上互补，让农村居民享受到与城镇居民同样的文明和福利。只有大力推进城乡一体化，我国整个城乡经济才能实现全面、可持续发展。
\end{question}

\begin{solution}

Urban-rural integration marks a new stage in my country's modernization and urbanization. Its objective is to dismantle the dual urban-rural economic structure—through comprehensive planning of urban and rural development, as well as through institutional reform and policy adjustments—thereby achieving policy parity between urban and rural areas and fostering complementary industrial development models. Ultimately, this aims to ensure that rural residents enjoy the same standards of civilization and social welfare as their urban counterparts. Only by vigorously advancing urban-rural integration can my country's entire urban and rural economy achieve comprehensive and sustainable development.
	
\end{solution}

\subsection{Unit 2}

\begin{question}{课后翻译}

水墨画（ink wash painting）是中国传统艺术形式之一，是中国国画（traditional Chinese painting）的代表。它大约始于唐代，兴盛（prosper）于宋代和元代。在其一千多年的历史中，水墨画经历了不断的发展、提高和完善。水墨画的主要创作工具和材料有毛笔、宣纸（rice paper）和墨，作品的艺术特点也与此紧密相关。水墨画在中国绘画史上具有很高的地位，甚至被认为是衡量东方绘画艺术水平的标准。
	
\end{question}

\begin{solution}

Ink wash painting is one of the traditional art forms of China and serves as a quintessential representative of traditional Chinese painting. It originated approximately during the Tang Dynasty and flourished throughout the Song and Yuan Dynasties. Over the course of its history—spanning more than a thousand years—ink wash painting has undergone continuous development, refinement, and perfection. The primary tools and materials used in its creation include the brush, *Xuan* paper (rice paper), and ink; consequently, the artistic characteristics of these works are inextricably linked to these very elements. Ink wash painting occupies a highly esteemed position in the history of Chinese art and is even regarded as a benchmark for evaluating the artistic caliber of Eastern painting.

\end{solution}

\subsection{Unit 3}

\begin{question}{课后翻译}
	
随着社会和经济的飞速发展，中国在国际舞台上发挥着越来越大的作用。在此背景下，我们必须坚守中华文化立场，加快构建中国话语和中国叙事体系（China's discourse and narrative systems），讲好中国故事、传播好中国声音，展现可信、可爱、可敬的中国形象。加强国际传播能力建设，全面提升国际传播效能，形成同我国综合国力和国际地位相匹配（be commensurate with）的国际话语权。深化文明交流互鉴，推动中华文化更好走向世界。
	
\end{question}


\begin{solution}

Amidst rapid social and economic development, China is playing an increasingly prominent role on the international stage. Against this backdrop, we must steadfastly uphold the stance of Chinese culture and accelerate the construction of China's discourse and narrative systems. We must tell China's story well and effectively convey its voice, thereby presenting a credible, amiable, and respectable image of the country. We must strengthen our capacity for international communication, comprehensively enhance its effectiveness, and establish an international voice commensurate with our comprehensive national strength and global standing. Furthermore, we must deepen exchanges and mutual learning among civilizations, thereby facilitating the broader global reach of Chinese culture.

\end{solution}

\subsection{Unit 4}
\begin{question}{课后翻译}
	
改革开放以来，中国政府采取了一系列改善生态环境、保护生物多样性的措施。许多重点生态工程得到推进或实施，一大批自然保护区（nature reserve）、生态示范区（ecological demonstration zone）和森林公园先后被设立。今后，我们还需要进一步提升生态系统的多样性、稳定性、持续性，牢固树立和践行绿水青山就是金山银山的理念，站在人与自然和谐共生的高度谋划发展。
	
\end{question}

\begin{solution}
	
Since the launch of the Reform and Opening-up policy, the Chinese government has adopted a series of measures to improve the ecological environment and conserve biodiversity. Numerous key ecological projects have been advanced or implemented, and a large number of nature reserves, ecological demonstration zones, and forest parks have been established in succession. Moving forward, we must continue to enhance the diversity, stability, and sustainability of our ecosystems; firmly uphold and put into practice the philosophy that "lucid waters and lush mountains are invaluable assets"; and plan our development from the perspective of harmonious coexistence between humanity and nature.
\end{solution}

\subsection{Unit 5}

\begin{question}{课后翻译}
	
中国载人航天工程（manned space program）于1992年开始实施，迄今已取得了巨大成就。2003年，“神舟五号”载人飞船（manned spacecraft）成功发射，使中国成为世界上继俄罗斯和美国之后第三个有能力独自将人送上太空的国家。2005年至今，中国又完成了多次神舟飞船载人发射。此外，通过研发空间实验室和无人货运飞船（unmanned cargo spacecraft），不断推动空间站建设，中国具备了长期开展在轨科学实验和综合开发利用太空资源的能力。
	
\end{question}

\begin{solution}
	
China's Manned Space Program was initiated in 1992 and has since achieved tremendous success. In 2003, the successful launch of the *Shenzhou-5* manned spacecraft made China the third nation in the world—following Russia and the United States—capable of independently sending humans into space. Since 2005, China has successfully conducted numerous additional manned missions using the *Shenzhou* spacecraft series. Furthermore, through the development of space laboratories and unmanned cargo spacecraft—thereby continuously advancing the construction of its space station—China has acquired the capability to conduct long-term scientific experiments in orbit and to comprehensively develop and utilize space resources.
	
\end{solution}

\subsection{Unit 6}

\begin{question}{课后翻译}

博鳌亚洲论坛（Boao Forum for Asia, or BFA）是一个总部设在中国海南省博鳌镇的国际组织。它由29个成员国共同发起，于2001年正式成立。论坛每年定期在博鳌举行会议，目前已成为亚洲以及其他洲政界、工商界和学术界领袖商讨亚洲及全球关键问题的高层次对话平台（high-level dialogue platform）。在新的历史时期，博鳌亚洲论坛将继续为亚洲及世界和平、繁荣与可持续发展做出积极贡献。

\end{question}

\begin{solution}
	
The Boao Forum for Asia (BFA) is an international organization headquartered in Boao, Hainan Province, China. Jointly initiated by 29 member states, it was officially established in 2001. The Forum holds regular annual meetings in Boao and has evolved into a high-level dialogue platform where leaders from the political, business, and academic spheres—spanning Asia and other continents—convene to discuss key regional and global issues. In this new historical era, the Boao Forum for Asia will continue to make positive contributions to peace, prosperity, and sustainable development in Asia and the world.
	
\end{solution}
